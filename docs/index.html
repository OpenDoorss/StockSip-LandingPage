Frequently Asked Questions

How does this app help me manage my liquor store? ▼
Our app allows you to keep real-time inventory control, record sales, low stock alerts, and optimize your orders to suppliers.
Can I receive notifications when a product runs out? ▼
Yes, you will receive automatic alerts when a product is about to run out, allowing you to make timely decisions and avoid stockouts.
Does the app allow generating sales and stock reports? ▼
Yes, you can generate detailed reports on sales, profit margins, best-selling products, and inventory turnover analysis.
Is it difficult to set up the app for my business? ▼
No, the setup is straightforward and intuitive. Additionally, we offer support and tutorials so you can start using it without complications.
